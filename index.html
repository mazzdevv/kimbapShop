We are a small mom and pop shop.

Our family moved from Seoul 10 years ago. We wanted to bring the taste of kimpab from our childhoods here for our children to experience. This is the authentic experience.

We only sell kimbap here! Kimbap is like the Korean version of sushi. Traditional kimbap is made of a variety of veggies (fresh, cooked, and/or pickled) wrapped in rice and seaweed to form a nice roll.

We are open 7:00AM to 6:00PM every day.
